Welcome to the Gift-a-Cheer Website

We have embarked on Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) for this year's work deliverables.
OKRs help provide clarity on our priorities and reduce abortive work. Achieving the agreed OKRs, however, is not a walk in the park.
We need these four superpowers: Focus, Align, Track and Stretch. We will also need our ABCs: Agility, Boldness and Courage.
To do so, we all need some doses of cheer from time to time, so let's Gift-a-Cheer to each other in our OKR journey!
